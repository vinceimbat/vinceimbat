Laboy
- Drift

Libot
- To walk around, usually with a clear idea where one is going.

Lakad
- The physical act.
- Moving from point A to point B (i.e., to achieve something)

Lakbayan
- Walking as a group with a protest

Alay Lakad
- Catholic practice of offering a walk.

Peteken (Pang.)
- To cross a field
