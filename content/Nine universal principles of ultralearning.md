[[ultralearning|Ultralearning]] has the following nine universal principles:

1. [[metalearning]]
2. Focus
3. Directness
4. Drill
5. Retrieval
6. Feedback
7. Retention
8. Intuition
9. Experimentation

These principles should be treated as flexible guidelines, not rigid rules.
