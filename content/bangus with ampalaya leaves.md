# Ingredients

- Bangus
- Ginger (grated ginger)
- Garlic (pressed, 3-4 cloves)
- Tomatoes (chop)
- Salt
- Water

# Steps

1. Make soup by mixing ginger, garlic, and tomatoes (5 minutes)
2. Drop bangus and cook for five minutes.
3. Put ampalaya leaves (30-45 minutes)
4. Cook with own heat.
